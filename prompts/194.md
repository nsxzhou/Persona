# 194. 废柴流逆袭节奏表 (退婚流)

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "outline_underdog_rise",
  "start": "MC is crippled/talentless, fiancée breaks engagement",
  "turning_point": "Finds a ring with a grandpa soul",
  "training": "Hellish suffering in the mountains",
  "comeback": "Returns to clan tournament after 3 years",
  "climax": "Defeats the ex-fiancée in one move",
  "quote": "Do not bully the young for being poor!"
}
```

## 使用场景
玄幻/修仙。经典的废柴退婚打脸流程。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **压抑**：前期压抑得越狠（受尽羞辱），后期的爆发越爽。
2.  **三年之约**：设立一个明确的时间节点（三年后），制造紧迫感和期待感。

## 示例输入
无。
