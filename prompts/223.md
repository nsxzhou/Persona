# 223. 破镜重圆：久别重逢场景

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "write_reunion_scene",
  "time_apart": "5 years / 10 years",
  "setting": "Airport / High school reunion / Business meeting",
  "mc_status": "Successful but lonely",
  "ex_status": "Married (fake) / Single parent / Amnesiac",
  "reaction": "Trying to act cool but hands are shaking",
  "dialogue": "'Long time no see. You look... good.'"
}
```

## 使用场景
言情/都市文。描写前任重逢时的情感张力。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **物是人非**：对比过去和现在的变化（外貌、身份、气质）。
2.  **克制**：表面云淡风轻，内心波涛汹涌，这种反差最动人。

## 示例输入
无。
