# 237. 恐怖无限流：生路提示设计

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "design_horror_clue",
  "danger": "Ghost kills anyone who makes a sound",
  "clue": "A painting of a zipped mouth / A diary entry about silence",
  "misdirection": "Players think they need to scream to scare it away",
  "realization": "MC realizes the pattern of deaths linked to noise"
}
```

## 使用场景
无限流/惊悚文。设计副本的生路和死路。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **规则致死**：鬼不可战胜，只能靠遵守/利用规则生存。
2.  **试错**：通过配角的死亡验证规则，增加紧迫感。

## 示例输入
无。
