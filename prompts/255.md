# 255. 娱乐圈：颁奖典礼获奖感言

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "write_award_speech",
  "award": "Best Actor / Best Newcomer",
  "context": "MC was boycotted by the industry previously",
  "speech_style": "Humble yet sharp / Sarcastic thanking of haters",
  "key_line": "'To those who said I couldn't make it... watch me.'",
  "reaction": "Thunderous applause / Haters' faces turning pale"
}
```

## 使用场景
娱乐明星文。打脸爽点的高潮时刻。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **暗讽**：感言中不带脏字地回击当年的打压者。
2.  **格局**：展现出主角的格局和从容，赢得路人缘。

无。
