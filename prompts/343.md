# 343. 细纲：期待感管理 (Expectation Management)

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "manage_reader_expectation",
  "promise": "MC will duel the Sect Leader in 3 days",
  "buildup": [
    "Day 1: MC trains a secret technique (Hope)",
    "Day 2: Sect Leader shows overwhelming power (Doubt/Fear)",
    "Day 3 (Morning): MC is calm, everyone thinks he will lose (Suspense)"
  ],
  "payoff": "MC wins in one move (Surprise/Relief)",
  "hook": "The Sect Leader smiles before falling (New Mystery)"
}
```

## 使用场景
细纲/节奏控制。通过“立Flag”和“压抑”来增强爽感。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **承诺**：明确告诉读者即将发生什么（决斗、考试、拍卖）。
2.  **阻碍**：在结果到来前，不断制造困难和悬念。

## 示例输入
规划“主角参加炼丹大会夺冠”的三天期待感铺垫。
