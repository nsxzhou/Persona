# 346. 细纲：多视角切换逻辑 (POV Switching)

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "plan_pov_switch",
  "main_event": "The Siege of the Capital",
  "povs": [
    {"character": "MC (General)", "focus": "Strategy, Command, Big Picture"},
    {"character": "Soldier A (Frontline)", "focus": "Horror, Blood, Chaos"},
    {"character": "Emperor (Palace)", "focus": "Anxiety, Politics, Betrayal"}
  ],
  "transition": "Cut from General ordering a charge -> Soldier hearing the horn",
  "goal": "Show the scale and complexity of the war"
}
```

## 使用场景
细纲/战争/群像。通过不同视角展现事件的全貌。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **互补**：每个视角看到的东西必须是不一样的，互为补充。
2.  **衔接**：视角切换要流畅，利用声音或动作作为转场。

## 示例输入
描写一场“丧尸围城”，分别用主角（指挥）、平民（逃生）、丧尸王（捕猎）三个视角。
