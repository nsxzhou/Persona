# 350. 细纲：全书核心梗概提炼 (Logline/Hook)

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "create_story_logline",
  "elements": {
    "Protagonist": "A blind swordsman",
    "Goal": "Reclaim his family's honor",
    "Antagonist": "A corrupt empire",
    "Stakes": "The fate of all martial arts"
  },
  "template": "When [Inciting Incident], a [Protagonist] must [Goal] before [Stakes], or else [Bad Outcome].",
  "result": "When his master is murdered, a blind swordsman must dismantle a corrupt empire to reclaim his honor before the ancient arts are lost forever."
}
```

## 使用场景
细纲/选题/简介。用一句话讲清楚故事核心，检验核心梗是否吸引人。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **简洁**：不超过50个字。
2.  **冲突**：必须包含主角、目标、阻碍和代价。

## 示例输入
为一本“重生回到高考前”的都市重生文写一句话梗概。
