# 357. 流程：心理活动具象化 (Inner Monologue)

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "visualize_inner_thought",
  "emotion": "Anxiety / Fear of failure",
  "abstract_thought": "He was worried he would lose.",
  "concrete_image": "His stomach tied in knots / The sword felt heavy in his hand / Sweat stung his eyes",
  "flashback": "Remembering his father's disappointed face"
}
```

## 使用场景
写作/心理。将抽象的心理活动转化为具象的身体反应或画面。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **Show, Don't Tell**：不要说“他很害怕”，要说“他的手在发抖”。
2.  **联想**：心理活动往往伴随着回忆或幻想。

## 示例输入
具象化描写：主角在表白前的紧张心理。
