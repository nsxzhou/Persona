# 373. 润色：回忆杀插入技巧 (Flashback Insertion)

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "insert_flashback_smoothly",
  "current_scene": "MC looks at a broken sword.",
  "memory_scene": "The day his master died giving him the sword.",
  "trigger": "The rust on the blade / A specific smell",
  "transition_in": "The noise of the battle faded, replaced by the sound of rain...",
  "transition_out": "A loud crash brought him back to reality.",
  "emotional_link": "Regret -> Determination"
}
```

## 使用场景
写作/情感。自然地插入回忆，避免打断当前节奏。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **触发物**：必须有一个具体的物品或感觉触发回忆（普鲁斯特效应）。
2.  **短小精悍**：回忆不要太长，点到为止。

## 示例输入
在主角面对强敌时，插入一段“当年特训”的回忆。
