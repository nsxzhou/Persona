# 375. 类型：剧本杀/谋杀之谜设计 (Murder Mystery Game)

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "design_murder_mystery_script",
  "setting": "1920s Shanghai / Cyberpunk Club / Medieval Castle",
  "victim": "The Gang Boss",
  "suspects": [
    {"role": "The Dancer", "motive": "Revenge for sister"},
    {"role": "The Bodyguard", "motive": "Debt"},
    {"role": "The Rival", "motive": "Power"}
  ],
  "timeline": "Detailed schedule of everyone from 8 PM to 10 PM",
  "clues": "Physical evidence + Testimony contradictions",
  "truth": "The Bodyguard did it, but the Dancer thinks she did."
}
```

## 使用场景
悬疑/无限流/综艺。设计一个完整的剧本杀案件。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **时间线**：每个人的时间线必须严丝合缝，但又有视差。
2.  **误导**：每个人都有不可告人的秘密（红鲱鱼）。

## 示例输入
设计一个发生在“暴风雪山庄”的五人剧本杀。
