# 95. 替身文学情感爆发点

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "write_stand_in_climax",
  "trope": "Stand-in (MC was a replacement for White Moonlight)",
  "trigger": "White Moonlight returns",
  "mc_action": "Leaves without a word / Throws money at Male Lead",
  "male_lead_reaction": "Realizes he loves the MC, not the Moonlight",
  "dialogue": "'I was never her. And you never loved me.'"
}
```

## 使用场景
虐恋/替身文。描写替身觉醒、渣男后悔的高潮情节。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **决绝**：主角离开时必须干脆利落，不拖泥带水。
2.  **错位**：渣男意识到真相时，主角已经不在乎了，制造爽感。

## 示例输入
无。
